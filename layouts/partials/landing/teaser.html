LinkAce is a self-hosted tool for effortlessly archiving, organizing, and sharing your favorite web links. With a clean interface, you can save articles, bookmark tools, and preserve important content long-term. Easily categorize and retrieve your links, or share your collection with others.

Tags & Lists

Choose tags to categorize links, add them to custom lists to group them by a topic or occasion.

{{ partial "icons/users.html" }}

Multiple Users + SSO

LinkAce supports multiple users and even offers Single Sign On via OAuth or OIDC. Links, lists and tags can be shared amongst the users or used together.

{{ partial "icons/monitor.html" }}

Continuous Monitoring

All saved links are checked for their availability on a regular basis. You get a notification if a website was moved or became unavailable.

{{ partial "icons/shield.html" }}

Automatic Archiving

After you saved a link, it will automatically saved by the Internet Archive. A reliable backup without maintenance. LinkAce will also monitor your links and notify you, if something's wrong.

{{ partial "icons/search.html" }}

Search, filter, order

A powerful search helps you find the links you are looking for. Filter and order the results by various properties.

{{ partial "icons/language.html" }}

Available in 15 Languages

Manage your links in English, German, Frensh, Japanese, Spanish, Polish, Chinese and more languages.

{{ partial "icons/zapier-logomark.html" }}

Integration with Zapier

Connect your LinkAce with over 5000 other applications like Google Drive, Discord or Slack through Zapier.

{{ partial "icons/api.html" }}

REST API

LinkAce offers a REST API for all features, so you can control and access all your data from other applications.

{{ partial "icons/user-unlock.html" }}

Guest Mode & Privacy

You can allow guests to view your link collection. Access can be controlled for each link, tag and list separately.
